My pet

Mouse lemur

It has been about three months I have got my pet. It is a mouse lemur. His name is chip. He is a super cute pet.

After having a summer holiday in my village, I have taken this mouse lemur with me here in mahanoro. I have never had a mouse lemur as a pet like this before. I check him three times a day during which I give him his food. I have learned many things about mouse lemur since I have got this pet.

I am looking forward to see my little chip growing up.
