My village

Anosibe An' Ala

Where is the village and how do we get there?

Anosibe An’Ala is the name of my village. It is a District in Alaotra mangoro region, Toamasina province. The way goes to my village is passing Moramanga and it’ s 75 km from there going to the south. Once in a blue moon you can go there by just simple car but most of the time we use to go by trucks because that road is one of the worst roads in Madagascar.

How does it look like?

This village is a green place decorated by forest and it is surrounded by two rivers called Manambolo and Mahamavo. Those two rivers are separated when passing the village but they are combined into one at the end of the village.

How is lives there?

Most of the people are farmer in this village for those people, they spend most of their times a year farming, harvesting then selling their products when they are ready.Apart from those mentioned before, there are other kinds of jobs that people do such as doctor, teaching, business, vet, preaching, etc.

People:

People are friendly, sociable and open with other people. If you are a stranger, they are welcoming people. Those are the reasons why almost all people from all tribe in Madagascar live in there.
